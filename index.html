What we do

We donate items to vulnerable communities like slums Spreading the Love of Jesus Christ to all.

Community Outreach

Visiting the elderly

The family organises outreaches in different societies that need extra attention and the people in it like the slums. During the outreaches, they visit different categories of people for example the elderly as seen in the photo above. They donate a few items like food, clothes, pads, soap, sugar and many others to help out in their daily life.

We Transform

Visiting orphanages

Extending love to orphans and street children

Every year, the family visits atleast two orphanages or street children's homes to extend love and care to the children in those homes. They donate very many basic materials but mainly scholastic materials needed by the children to promote their education. 'Little children need love and its our role as a family to love and stand with them all the way through until they become what their dreams say'.

Integrity

Personal Outreach

The family helps people grow financially

The family insists on having transformed lives. Every year, the family supports people who are unable to sustain their lives financially through contributing to their capital to start their own business that can help them grow in all aspects.
